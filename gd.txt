
GAME PLAN

CONCEPT:

Simple 3d game where we will have a player and the enemy the player has to collect the collectables and using the collected bone the player will attack the enemy if the player killed the enemy the player will regain the health power .
the enemy will chase the player if he collide with the enemy
if the player move fast and exited the circle the enemy will go to this original position .


PLAYER

ROLE:
will collect the bones

attack the enemy using the collected bones

ACTIVITIES:

RUN 
JUMP
ATTACK

ENEMY

ROLE:

Chase the player

Attack the player

ACTIVITIES:

MOVE AROUND 
CHASE THE PLAYER

COMMON THINGS:

Both the player and enemy will have a health bar
if the player health bar is empty the player will die the game will end
